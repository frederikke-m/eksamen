Stelton Classic & Cylinda Line

Task

To create a graphic series showcasing some of Danish design company Stelton’s most iconic products.

Challenge

The Classic and Cylinda Line was created in the late ‘60s-early 70’s, when minimalistic design was at it's height. As such, my design should express the elegance and simplicity of this mindset and the products it sparked.

Solution

To really highlight the product itself and it's functionality, this series is kept consistent in simple typography and a minimalistic setting. Everything unnecessary has been cut away to feature only the most essential of the products, and I believe Ad Reinhardt put it best when he said “Less is more.”

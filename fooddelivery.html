Food delivery app

Task

To create a food delivery app based on the wishes of one of our classmates.

Challenge

Basing the design off of one of my classmates meant it had to look clean, polished and organized. A plain, monochromatic black and white theme with a minimum of graphic elements and a generic sans-serif font seemed way too simple though.

Solution

The solution was therefore found in a grid-based layout divided into categories of cuisine, again split into popularity, price and distance. A simple yet memorable font combined with minimalistic linedrawings gave the design the desired touch of organized minimalism. To elevate the look and feel however, I decided to add the bold yellow for a pop of color. It was important though that it didn't distract from the true purpose of the app â€“ ordering food. So I used the yellow for functionality as well to highlight important aspects of the design and guide users through the app.

Installation project

Task

To create an interactive experience for the users of Økolariet in Vejle.

Challenge

Økolariet is already an established organisation with a clientbase that carries great expectations. As such, standards were high, and it was important that our team's product was top-modern and as user-centered as could be, all the while keeping in line with Økolariets image and mission of promoting climate and nature awareness.

Solution

We found the solution in creating a scenery of an Antarctic landscape that could be experienced through a virtual reality headset. The technology was chosen based on the teenage section of Økolariet’s target audience, which they admitted they didn't always quite manage to capture, and choosing an intriguing and extremely current trend inside techonology meant a higher chance of getting to this specific part of the audience. The scenery is created in a simple, geometric style with flat, bright or saturated colors and shadows, and the same thing goes for the video and poster presenting our concept. Climate change is a heavy subject to present, especially to kids and young adults, which is why we chose to lighten the feel around our product – because we believe that guests should leave the experience feeling enlightened and motivated to make a change, not dispirited and hopeless.

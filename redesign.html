Redesign of website

Task

To pick out an exisiting website that looked like it could do with an upgrade.

Challenge

My team and I chose to give a much needed facelift to lejren.dk run by the organization Børnelejren på Langeland. This organization works to give underprivileged kids a well deserved break from everyday life by arranging summer camps in Southern Langeland. A truly wonderful cause – unfortunately their website didn’t quite express this. One of the greater challenges of the site was it's messiness. It was hard to find anything, especially how to support and contact the organization. As an institution that relies heavily on sponsors and donations, this was a critical point.

Solution

So we started out by distinctively dividing the content into four main pages: About Børnelejren, for insititutions, how to support and contact. This content was then put into a design that clearly reflected Børnelejren’s values. A serious yet soft typography was used, that both future sponsors as well as kids and institutions can see themselves in, and this typography expressed itself in bold headers and clear call-to-actions, all to minimize the confusion of the original site. Combined with a lively turqouise, a peachy orange and cutesy illustrations, the site now radiates life and happiness to a much higher degree than before.
